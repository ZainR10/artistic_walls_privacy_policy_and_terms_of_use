1. Privacy Policy

I respect your privacy and am committed to protecting the personal data I collect.

Information I Collect

  • Personal Data: Your Name, Email Address, and Profile Image are collected through Google Sign-In (Firebase Authentication) to enable account features and sync your Favorites and subscription status.
  • Purchase Data: When you make an in-app purchase, I receive and store a purchase token, product ID, and subscription expiry date from Google Play. This is used solely to verify and grant your subscription access. I do not store your payment card details — all billing is handled entirely by Google Play.
  • Device Identifiers: Firebase UID is used to manage your session and ensure app security.
  • Usage Data: I do not collect behavioral analytics or tracking data beyond what Firebase Authentication requires.

Data Security

All data is transmitted securely using HTTPS and stored on Cloudflare's infrastructure. Purchase tokens are validated server-side via the Google Play Developer API and never exposed to any third party.

Third-Party Services

I use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication & session management
  • Google Play Billing — In-app purchase processing and subscription management
  • Google AdMob — Advertising (only shown to non-subscribed users)
  • Cloudflare Workers & D1 — Backend API and database
  • Cloudflare R2 — Wallpaper image storage

I do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third parties.

Data Retention & Deletion

I retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request data deletion directly within the app settings or via our Account Deletion page. All deletion requests are processed within 7 days of verification.

Billing & Renewal

  • All purchases are processed by Google Play. Prices are shown in your local currency before purchase.
  • Subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing period unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the renewal date.
  • Your Google Play account is charged within 24 hours before the renewal date.

Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time through the Google Play Store:

  1. Open Google Play Store → tap your profile icon
  2. Go to Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions
  3. Find Artistic Walls and tap Cancel subscription

Or visit: play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions

Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. You retain access until then.

Refund Policy

Since all billing is handled by Google Play, refund requests must be submitted directly to Google. I do not independently process refunds. To request a refund:

  • Visit Google Play's refund request page
  • Or contact Google Play Support directly
⚠️ Note on Account Deletion & Subscriptions: Deleting your Artistic Walls account removes your purchase records from our servers but does not cancel your active Google Play subscription. You must cancel through Google Play separately to stop future charges.

Access After Subscription Ends

When a subscription expires or is cancelled, premium wallpapers and ad-free features will no longer be accessible. Non-premium wallpapers remain freely available.
